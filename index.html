The 10-Minute Upgrade Ramen

Instant ramen, but we drop in a poached egg, green onions, and a splash of sesame oil to make it feel like real food.

Gamified Rescue Steps:

  1. Boil & Drop: Bring water to a boil, drop the noodles, and throw the spice packet away or use half to control sodium.
  2. Add Reinforcements: Crack an egg directly into the boiling broth in the last 2 minutes. Do not stir it; let it poach!
  3. Plating Glory: Pour into a bowl, snip green onions with scissors (no chopping board to wash!), and add a drizzle of soy sauce or hot sauce.
  4. Immediate Clean: Rinse the pot *immediately* after pouring. Do not let ramen starch dry. Done!
